What does an AI workflow audit cost?

The audit is $750. It includes a 45-minute discovery call, a written report with specific recommendations, and a 30-minute review call to walk through it together.

How long does an audit take?

About two weeks from booking to delivery. Most of that time is me analyzing what we talked about and building your report, not you waiting around.

What if the audit doesn't find anything useful?

Then you don't pay. If I can't identify at least 5 hours a week in real, actionable savings, it's a full refund within 7 days.

Who is this for?

Small business owners, usually with a handful of employees, who feel like they're drowning in repetitive admin work. Scheduling, invoicing, follow-up, that kind of thing.

Do I need to already understand AI to work with you?

No. That's actually the point. I translate what's possible into what you should actually do, in plain terms.
